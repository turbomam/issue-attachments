# Lexar 64GB microSDXC Specs

- **Model:** Lexar 64 GB microSDXC I V30 1066x U3 A2
- **Capacity:** 64 GB
- **Type:** microSDXC (eXtended Capacity, 32GB to 2TB)
- **Bus Interface:** UHS-I (Ultra High Speed - First Gen)
- **Video Speed Class:** V30 (Minimum 30 MB/s sustained write speed, ideal for 4K video)
- **Marketing Speed:** 1066x (Up to ~160 MB/s read speed)
- **UHS Speed Class:** U3 (Minimum 30 MB/s write speed)
- **Application Class:** A2 (Optimized for running apps directly from storage with high random read/write speeds)
